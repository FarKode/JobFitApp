Overview & Commitment

Effective Date: July 15, 2026

JobFit ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile application designed to help users create professional resumes and check their ATS (Applicant Tracking System) compatibility scores — all entirely offline.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information (if any) is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding your personal data when using the JobFit application.

Key Principle: JobFit is built as an offline-first application. Your resume data, personal information, and all content you create within the app are stored exclusively on your device and are never transmitted to any server owned or operated by us.

2

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account registration — the app does not require sign-up, login, or any form of authentication.
  • No personal data transmission — your name, email, phone number, work history, education, skills, and any other resume content you enter remain solely on your device.
  • No analytics or tracking — we do not use any first-party analytics, crash reporting, or telemetry services.
  • No cookies or local identifiers — the app does not use cookies, fingerprinting, or device identifiers for tracking purposes.
3

Local Data Storage

All data you create in JobFit — including resume profiles, personal information, work experience, education, skills, projects, and certifications — is stored locally on your device using Hive, a lightweight and fast key-value database that runs entirely within your device's application sandbox.

This data is protected by the Android operating system's built-in application sandboxing, which prevents other applications from accessing JobFit's data.

  • Data is stored in your device's private app directory.
  • No cloud backup or synchronization is performed by the app.
  • You can clear all data at any time via Settings → Clear All Data within the app.
4

Third-Party Services

JobFit integrates the following third-party service:

Google AdMob (by Google LLC)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements within the app (banner ads, interstitial ads, and rewarded ads). Google AdMob may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Device information (device model, operating system version)
  • Advertising ID (Google Advertising ID on Android)
  • IP address (for approximate geolocation and ad targeting)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)
  • App usage data related to ad display timing

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to or control over the data that Google collects through AdMob.

Note: You may opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

Google Fonts

The app uses Google Fonts for typography. Font files may be fetched from Google's servers when first used. Google may log standard web request data (IP address, user agent) during font delivery. Refer to Google Fonts Privacy FAQ for details.

5

App Permissions

JobFit requests the following Android permissions:

  • INTERNET — Required for displaying advertisements via Google AdMob, downloading optional ATS keyword database updates, and loading Google Fonts. This permission is not used to transmit any of your personal or resume data.

The app does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, files, or any other sensitive device resources.

6

Data Sharing & Transfer

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties.

Since all resume data is stored locally on your device, there is no data to share. The only data that leaves your device is the standard advertising data collected by Google AdMob as described in Section 4.

When you use the "Share" or "Export PDF" features, you are choosing to share your generated resume file through your device's native sharing mechanism. This is entirely under your control.

7

Children's Privacy

JobFit is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the app does not collect any personal information from any user, this concern is inherently mitigated by our offline-first architecture.

8

Data Retention & Deletion

All data created within JobFit exists solely on your device. We do not retain any copies of your data on remote servers.

You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Using Settings → Clear All Data within the app to delete all resumes and preferences.
  • Uninstalling the JobFit application, which removes all associated app data from your device.
  • Clearing app data via your device's system settings: Settings → Apps → JobFit → Storage → Clear Data.
9

Security Measures

We take reasonable measures to protect the information stored within the app:

  • Application Sandboxing: Android's built-in sandbox isolates JobFit's data from other applications.
  • No Remote Storage: Since data never leaves your device, it cannot be intercepted during transmission.
  • Secure Connections: All network requests (ad loading, optional database updates) are made over HTTPS (TLS/SSL encrypted connections).
  • Code Obfuscation: Release builds employ R8 code shrinking and obfuscation to protect application logic.
